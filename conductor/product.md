# Initial Concept
DORA's public-facing knowledge site (dora.dev).

# Product Guide

## Target Users
Builders and leaders working in technology-driven teams and organizations.

## Goals
- Provide a comprehensive catalog of capabilities and research insights.
- Offer interactive assessment tools (like the Quick Check) for teams to measure their performance.
- Serve as the official repository for DORA's annual reports and resources.

## Key Features
- **Capability Catalog:** A searchable database of research-backed capabilities (e.g., Continuous Integration, Trunk-based Development).
- **Interactive Tools:** Interactive assessment tools like the "Quick Check" or "AI Model" for performance benchmarking.
- **Resource Library:** A library of publications, guides, and a decade of DORA research data.

## Success Metrics
- **User Engagement:** High user engagement with capability articles and research insights.
- **Assessment Completion:** The number of teams completing the Quick Check assessment.
- **Operational Efficiency:** Ease of content management and publishing for the DORA team.
